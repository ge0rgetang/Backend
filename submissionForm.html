App Tester


We're .native, a local startup looking for our second round of App Testers. Our free app is a local social network. We will pay you $50 to use our app for one week, on your time. Requirements include:

  • 2 posts/day and 1 reply/day to someone else's post (posts should be at least a few words)
  • Keep track of any bugs encountered, as well as providing a few sentences of feedback
  • Tell as many of your friends/frat brothers/sorority sisters/classmates as you can about us!

Additionally, some variation of iPhone (5, 6, or 7) is needed, as iOS 10 is required. Please use your berkeley.edu address in the email field as only Cal students may participate. You must also be in a frat/sorority.

If chosen, we will contact you through phone and email to set up a quick meeting near campus where we will introduce you to the app, go over specifics, and answer any questions. Thanks, and go bears!
